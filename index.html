This section is drafted from the details you provided and can be finalized once you confirm your eligibility rules, privacy language, and bureau options.

Who should apply

Students and qualified candidates interested in wildlife, fisheries, parks, conservation, public service, law enforcement support, natural resource management, and related fields.

What to submit

Complete the application form and upload a resume, letter of intent, recommendation letter, and transcript in PDF, DOC, or DOCX format.

How selections may be evaluated

Applications may be reviewed based on academic background, program fit, stated interests, bureau preferences, communication skills, and submitted materials.

Important dates

Spring: submit by November 1
Summer: submit by March 15
Fall: submit by May 1

These dates are shown without a year. The site logic uses the next upcoming annual deadline based on the visitor’s local date.
